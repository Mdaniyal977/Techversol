WHAT ARE AUGMENTED & VIRTUAL REALITIES?

Augmented and virtual realities are alternate digital realities developed using specialized software and implemented with the help of specialized hardware. A virtual reality (VR) is a digital realm free from the limitations of the natural laws of space (and physics). The immersive experience it delivers with the help of a VR headset is highly engaging and takes the user to a new world. Augmented reality (AR) is where we augment the natural reality with digital objects displayed with the help of mobile phones, other screens, or specially built glasses.

BEYOND THE VISIBLE

With AR, you can build digital assets hidden within our reality while VR allows you to build a new reality altogether. Each has a number of benefits and can change lives forever, enabling professionals to get technical trainings in life-like digital environments, and optimize process and cut costs for businesses, among other priceless advantages.
